# Shopee Expense Calculator Extension 🛒💰

A powerful Chrome Extension to easily calculate your total expenses on Shopee. It automatically crawls through your past orders, summarizes your total spending, and lists out your purchases—all within your browser!

![Extension Icon](shopee-favicon.png)

## Features ✨
- **1-Click Calculation:** Instantly start calculating your total spending on Shopee.
- **Save & Resume:** Orders are saved locally. If you accidentally close the popup or page, the calculation can resume exactly from where it left off.
- **Detailed List:** View a chronological list of all your purchased items along with their prices.
- **Global Support:** Automatically detects your Shopee region and uses the correct currency (Supports `ID`, `MY`, `SG`, `PH`, `TH`, `VN`, `TW`, `BR`, `MX`, `CO`, `CL`).
- **Privacy First:** All data is calculated locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers.

## 🛠️ Installation Guide

Because this extension is not yet published on the Chrome Web Store, you can install it manually using "Developer Mode".

### Step 1: Download the Extension
Download the extension as a ZIP file by clicking the link below, then unzip/extract it to a folder you can easily find.

[Download Shopee Expense Extension (ZIP)](https://github.com/novintriantonius/shopee-expense-extension/archive/refs/heads/main.zip)

### Step 2: Open Chrome Extensions Page
1. Open Google Chrome.
2. Type `chrome://extensions/` in the URL bar and press **Enter**.

### Step 3: Enable Developer Mode
In the top-right corner of the Extensions page, toggle the switch that says **Developer mode** to **ON**.

### Step 4: Load the Extension
1. Click the **Load unpacked** button that appears in the top-left corner.
2. Select the folder where you cloned/extracted this repository (the folder containing `manifest.json`).
3. You should now see the "Shopee Order Calculator" extension listed and its icon in your browser toolbar!

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Navigate to your local Shopee website (e.g., `https://shopee.co.id`, `https://shopee.com.my`, etc.).
2. Make sure you are **logged into** your Shopee account.
3. Click the Shopee Expense Calculator icon in your Chrome toolbar.
4. Click **Start Calculate**. 
5. Wait for the magic to happen! You will see the total number of orders climbing. Once complete, it will display your Grand Total and a list of items.
   - *Tip: If you have thousands of orders, it might take a minute. If you close the popup, just open it again and click "Continue Calculate".*

---

## 🧹 Clearing Data
If you switch accounts or want to recalculate from zero, simply open the extension and click the **Clear Data** button.
